Courses


Barista Training

Himalayan Java Barista Coffee School was introduced to promote the culture of vocational training in Nepal. We believe vocational training helps individuals to understand the essential elements of a particular course without wasting much time or energy, compared to a full-fledged university degree. We also believe that vocational training is the backbone of all developing countries..
