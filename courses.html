Courses


Barista Training

Simran's Confectionery is very famous when it comes to Barista Training. As soon as you complete your schooling , you can come to learn with us.Our well-trained staffs with more than 8 years of experience will help you master the art of coffee.This training helps you to prepare many mocktail drinks and varieties of hot and cold coffee. We have 10 days class which includes Coffee tasting session and Latte Art session with our top trainers from USA. For those people who want to learn this course really quick , 5 days private course is also available for them.If you want a long time course, you can join our 3 months premium class that will include Theory class,Grinding and Brewing session,Coffee Bean Grading session,Milk steaming and many more.Timing for the courses are different and adjustable.Most of our students are working at the top hotels throughout the world so do not miss this chance.




Cake Baking and Decorating

Simran's Confectionery serves you with 6 months of baking course which includes the theory class where you will be taught about the basic ingredients of baking, the chemical properties of ingredients and its uses. Moreover, students will be participating in Cake Tasting Ceremony where they can explore varieties of mouthwatering desserts,pastries prepared by our mentors.Students will be taught with cake icing classes where they'll be learning about flower decorations,use of chocolates,fruits,nuts in the cake. Our main moto is to serve the best to our students who are willing to rule the cake industry.The best part about this course is that , our students will get a very good chance to visit more than 20 bakeries who have been serving since a long time.Isn't it interesting?The timing for this course is 10am to 5pm every Mon-Fri.After the completion of this course, each student will be preparing some new designs of wedding cake.From the best wedding cakes, two of them will be selected for cake competition that is going to held next year.




Chocolate and Waffles

Everyone loves chcolates and the food items made from chocolates.This course is mostly preferred by young people because we all know no one can say no to chocolates. In this 7 days course,you will be able to serve your own handmade chocolates to your family,friends and relatives.We will be teaching you how to make milk chocolates,dark chocolates,oats chocolates and coconut chocolates.Moreover,we all know that Belgium waffles are trending these days .If you also follow the trend and visit different stores for eating waffles then this course is best for you.We will teach you about the toppings used in waffles and basics of waffle making.
