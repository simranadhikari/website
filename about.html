About us


Welcome to our bakery!

Simran's Confectionery was founded in 2020 A.D by Ms.Simran Adhikari, with the aim of serving something yummy to all the people.It is a good place to learn culinary arts and enhance your skills. Our Bakery was awarded as the "Best Bakery and Training center of Nepal" in 2021 A.D. Simran's Confectionery was not just established to bake items and sell them but the main reason behind establishing it was to promote local level bakeries to a national level. Miss Simran loved to bake since she was 12, so she decided to open her own shop where she could encourage upcoming youths to learn and upskill themselves.

Simran's Confectionery serves you with over 50 varieties of cakes and pastries that will dissolve to your mouth.Our mouthwatering items are going to blow your taste buds.Many of the foreigners visit our place just to eat our one of the top seller item that is Choco Moose Cake.

We have served in more than thousand weddings,birthdays and parties.We are so glad to be a part of your happiness. Our main moto is to bring happiness in each and every faces. We are in collaboration with Kathmandu Fresh Bakery.We also provide many Barista and Baking courses that is affiliated to top university of U.S.A.

Mission and Vision

To bring happiness in each and every face
To serve good quality food items
To bring bakery/pastry as one of the main stream careers
To uplift small skills to represent globally
